Visualization of team slack communications for NIH National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences (NCATS) award project

The communication network on the left shows how team members interact with each other on slack for NIH NCATS award BioMedical Translator project. Link colors represent different types of communication. Node colors are Data Translator team colors. Refer to colored team member list for details on colored team members and institutions. Node size is proportional to the sum of the number of links and the number of broadcast messages associated with the node. You can pan, zoom, reset view, or drag a node to influence layout. You can also click a node or link to select it to see communication details in the info box below the visualization, click it again to deselect it or click another node or link to make other selections.

The word cloud on the right shows the 100 most frequently referenced words across all team communication messages on slack channels. The larger the words, the more frequently the words are referenced.
